Why SummiTest

Software testers are critical in application development. They are experts who offer quality assurance and put applications through the wringer to find out bugs, poor performance, and some interface issues. To find the flaws they run all kinds of tests; stress, performance, functional, scalability, user acceptance; at various stages of the software life cycle.

This training course focuses on understanding and developing various skills required by Quality Assurance and Software Testers, preparing to use various tools and techniques to check and make sure that defects are identified and corrected before software is released to users.
